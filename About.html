Our Story

Founded in 2014, Riddhi Motor Training School has established itself as one of Vadodara's most trusted driving education providers. Located conveniently in Maneja, we've trained thousands of students to become confident and responsible drivers.

Our school is fully licensed by the Regional Transport Office (RTO) and adheres to all government regulations for driver training. We take pride in our modern training methods, experienced instructors, and high success rate.

At Riddhi, we don't just teach you how to drive - we teach you how to drive safely and confidently in all road conditions.
